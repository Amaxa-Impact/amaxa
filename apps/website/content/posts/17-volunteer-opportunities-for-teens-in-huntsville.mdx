If you're a teenager living in Huntsville, Alabama, you are probably looking for ways to make a difference in your community. Huntsville is a vibrant city that values community involvement, and there are countless opportunities for teens to volunteer and give back.

Volunteering not only allows you to make a positive impact on your community, but it also provides you with valuable work experience and the chance to meet new people. It can be a truly rewarding experience that can help you develop important skills such as teamwork, leadership, and communication.

Whether you're passionate about animals, the environment, or helping people in need, Huntsville has a wide range of organizations that are always on the lookout for enthusiastic volunteers. By volunteering your time, you can make a real difference in the lives of those around you and contribute to the betterment of your community.

So, don't hesitate to explore the various volunteering opportunities available in Huntsville. You might find that it's the perfect way to spend your free time while making a positive impact on the world around you. With so many organizations to choose from, there's sure to be a cause that resonates with your interests and passions. Go ahead and get involved today!

## 1. [The Boys and Girls Club of North Alabama](https://cm.hsvchamber.org/list/member/boys-girls-clubs-of-north-alabama-294)

![Boys and Girls Club](assets/img/photos/alabama-boys-and-girls-club.png)
*Photo Credit: [Huntsville Madison County Chamber](https://cm.hsvchamber.org/list/member/boys-girls-clubs-of-north-alabama-294)*

The Boys and Girls Club of North Alabama is a non-profit organization that provides after-school and summer programs for youth in Huntsville. Their mission is to enable young people to reach their full potential as productive, caring, and responsible citizens.

As a volunteer, you can assist with tutoring, mentoring, and program delivery. You can help young people develop important academic and life skills, and serve as a positive role model and mentor for them.

[Volunteer at Boys and Girls Club](https://www.bgcnal.com/volunteer/)

## 2. [Huntsville Hospital](https://www.huntsvillehospital.org/)

![Huntsville Hospital](assets/img/photos/huntsville-hospital.png)
*Photo Credit: [Huntsville Hospital](https://careers.hhsys.org/allied-health)*

Huntsville Hospital is a leading medical facility located in Huntsville, Alabama. They offer a variety of volunteer opportunities for teenagers who are interested in gaining experience in the medical field, giving back to their community, and making a difference in the lives of patients and their families.

As a volunteer at Huntsville Hospital, you can assist with patient transport, perform clerical work, and help deliver flowers to patients. You will be given the opportunity to work alongside healthcare professionals and gain valuable insight into the inner workings of a hospital.

[Volunteer at Huntsville Hospital](https://www.huntsvillehospital.org/volunteer/)
